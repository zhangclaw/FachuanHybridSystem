### P1 — 类型安全与架构方向

#### Any 类型泄漏修复

`core/dependencies/automation_sms_wiring.py` 中 7 个工厂函数返回类型从 `-> Any` 改为对应 Protocol：
- `build_sms_case_service() -> ICaseService`
- `build_sms_client_service() -> IClientService`
- `build_sms_lawyer_service() -> ILawyerService`
- `build_sms_case_chat_service() -> ICaseChatService`
- `build_sms_case_log_service() -> ICaseLogService`
- `build_sms_document_processing_service() -> IDocumentProcessingService`
